Audi vs. Volvo - A Competitive Comparison

Brand Philosophy and Positioning

Audi

  • Technological advancement
  • Performance
  • Sophisticated design
  • Vorsprung Durch Technik
  • Commitment to innovation

Volvo

  • Safety reputation
  • Scandinavian design
  • Pragmatic luxury
  • Environmental consciousness
  • Family oriented
  • Thoughtful, durable vehicles with class leading safety

Design Language and Aesthetics

Audi

  • Clean, minimalist, agressive styling
  • Singleframe Grille
  • High quality interior materials
  • Technological approach (digital screens)
  • Emotionally driven designs, visually striking

Volvo

  • Minimalism with clean lines and functional elegance
  • Spaciousness, visibility, ergonomics
  • Less focused on visual appeal

Engineering Performance

Audi

  • Significant emphasis on performance engineering
  • QUATTRO ALL WHEEL DRIVE
  • Powerful engines
  • Advanced drivetrains
  • Sport-tuned suspension
  • Driver focus

Volvo

  • "Industry-leading" safety
  • Efficient powertrains, focus on sustainability
  • Comfort over sportiness

Safety Approach

Audi

  • Comprehensive safety systems
  • Preventative tech
  • Driver's assistance

Volvo

  • Invented 3 point seatbelt in 1959
  • Side impact protection, whiplash protection
  • Pedestrian detection
  • Goal to have zero people killed or injured in Volvos

History

Since we know Audi's history, let's skip to Volvo's.

  • Founded in 1927 in Sweden
  • Invented 3 point seatbelt in 1959
  • Lambda Sond (1976) 3-way catalytic converter with O2 sensor reduced emissions by 90%
  • Acquired by Ford Motor Company in 1999
  • Acquired by Geely in 2010 aka China
